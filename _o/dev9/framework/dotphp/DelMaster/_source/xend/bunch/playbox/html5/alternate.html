Inline formatting.

In this paragraph you will find inline elements such as em and strong. You will also find lesser used inline elements such as superscript, subscript and markup elements like cite and the newer mark element. Many of these will remain unstyled in frameworks, giving you the freedom to style them as you see fit, or allow the browser default styling to suffice. I'll also throw in ABBR as well.
